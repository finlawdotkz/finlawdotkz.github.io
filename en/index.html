About Us

LLP «Law Firm Financial Advocate» — a trusted partner since 2017

The company was founded in 2017 and specializes in comprehensive legal support for businesses and individuals. Our specialists are members of the Legal Consultants Chamber «Center for Legal Protection and Legal Services» and operate in strict accordance with the Law of the Republic of Kazakhstan «On Advocacy and Legal Assistance» and the Code of Professional Ethics.

Our team includes specialists with practical experience in judicial bodies, justice authorities, the National Bank of Kazakhstan, local executive bodies, and national companies. We handle cases in all 17 regions of Kazakhstan and provide full legal support — from initial consultation to enforcement of court rulings.

Licensed Advocates

All company specialists hold active advocate licenses, academic degrees, and professional certificates. Our activities are insured in accordance with legal requirements.

450+ Cases Won

Over 10+ years of practice, hundreds of cases have been successfully completed in arbitration, civil, and criminal courts. Clients include individuals, sole proprietors, small and large businesses.

We Work Across Kazakhstan

Our office is in Almaty, but we handle cases in all regions of the country — remotely and with on-site visits. Online consultations are available via WhatsApp and video calls.

Free Initial Consultation

You can receive a free initial legal consultation by phone or WhatsApp. Free assistance is also provided to socially vulnerable citizens.

Transparent Terms

The cost of services is discussed in advance and fixed in the contract. No hidden fees. A success fee is available for certain categories of cases.

Confidentiality

Attorney-client privilege is guaranteed by law. All information provided by the client is strictly protected and cannot be disclosed to third parties without your consent.
